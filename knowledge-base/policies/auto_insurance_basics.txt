Auto Insurance Policy Guidelines:

1. Policy Number Format:
   - Format: AUTO-XXXX-XXXX
   - Example: AUTO-1234-5678
   - Must be active policy

2. Coverage Periods:
   - Policies are valid for 12 months
   - Claims must be filed within 30 days of incident

3. Documentation Required for Accident related claims:
   - The claim form
   - Photo of damage
   - Repair estimate from approved shop
   - Police report 


4. Documentation Required for Theft related claims:
   - The claim form 
   - Police report
