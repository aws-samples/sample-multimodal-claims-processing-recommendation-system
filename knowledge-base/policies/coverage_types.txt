Auto Insurance Coverage Types:

1. Basic Coverage:
   - Deductible: $500
   - Maximum claim: $25,000
   - Covers: Basic accidents, theft
   
2. Premium Coverage:
   - Deductible: $250
   - Maximum claim: $50,000
   - Covers: All basic plus natural disasters

3. Minimum Coverage:
   - Deductible: $1,000
   - Maximum claim: $15,000
   - Covers: Only accident damage
