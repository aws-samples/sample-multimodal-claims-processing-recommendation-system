CLAIM TYPE IDENTIFICATION GUIDELINES

1. Accident Claims - Key Indicators:
   - Mention of collision or crash
   - Description of vehicle damage
   - Other vehicles involved
   - Police report with accident details
   - Repair estimates for damage
   - Photos showing vehicle damage

2. Theft Claims - Key Indicators:
   - Vehicle reported stolen
   - Last known location
   - Time vehicle discovered missing
   - Forced entry evidence
   - Police report with theft details
   - Personal items stolen from vehicle

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS BY CLAIM TYPE:
- ACCIDENT: claim form, police report, repair estimate, damage photos
- THEFT: claim form, police report

DOCUMENT TYPE IDENTIFICATION:
- claim form: filenames containing "claim", "sample", "form", "initial"
- police report: filenames containing "police", "report", "incident"  
- repair estimate: filenames containing "repair", "estimate", "mechanic", "quote"
- damage photos: image files (.jpg, .png, .jpeg) or filenames with "photo", "image"
