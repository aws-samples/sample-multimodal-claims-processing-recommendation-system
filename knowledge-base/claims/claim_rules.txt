Claim Validation Rules:

1. Required Information:
   - Policy number (AUTO-XXXX-XXXX)
   - Date of incident
   - Vehicle details (make, model, year)
   - Damage description
   - Required documents based on claim type

2. Claim Limits:
   - Minimum claim amount: $100
   - Maximum depends on coverage type
   - Must be filed within 30 days

3. Vehicle Information Required:
   - Vehicle Make
   - Vehicle Model
   - Year
   - VIN Number
