Claim Processing Steps:

1. Initial Validation:
   - Verify policy number format and status
   - Check incident date is within 30 days
   - Confirm all required fields present

2. Coverage Verification:
   - Check policy type
   - Verify claim amount within limits
   - Apply appropriate deductible

3. Documentation Check:
   - Check claim form for initial understanding of situation
   - Validate additional documents based on claim type (accident, theft)

4. Final Processing:
   - Calculate coverage amount
   - Apply deductible
   - Generate claim summary
